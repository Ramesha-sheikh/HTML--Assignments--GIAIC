What is Technology

Technology is the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes, making our lives more efficient, convenient, and connected. From the invention of the wheel to the latest advancements in artificial intelligence, technology continues to shape how we communicate, work, and live. It empowers businesses to grow, enhances our daily routines through smart devices, and has revolutionized industries such as healthcare, education, and transportation. With rapid advancements in areas like robotics, the Internet of Things (IoT), and blockchain, the future holds limitless possibilities, bringing us closer to a world where anything is possible with the right tools and innovation.
